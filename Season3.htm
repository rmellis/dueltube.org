Season 3


In the bugining Kaiba, Yugi, and their friends travel to KaibaCorp Duel Tower and find that the Big 5 from Season 1 have canceled their series. Yami then duels against Gansley. Meanwhile, Seto and Mokuba are shown memories from their past. Tea duels against Crump, a penguin-obsessed pedophile, while Seto sees memories from his childhood. Joey is put on trial and discovers who's been taking down all the abridged series. Tristan, Duke, and Serenity duel against Nesbitt, and Tristan gets into an unfortunate accident.

 
Read More Marik and friends try to come up with a plan to defeat Yugi. When Mokuba is kidnapped by Noah, Seto discovers a startling secret. Gozaburo later traps the heroes and Noah in a virtual labyrinth. some time later Tea and Serenity reminisce about previous episodes.
