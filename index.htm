What is DuelTube?


DuelTube is an organised library of all the best YuGiOh Abridged content!
It includes all 4 seasons and much more..

We embed the official YouTube videos uploaded by LittleKuriboh within DuelTube to ensure that he is credited for videos watched on this site. If for some reason that video cannot be played here then it will be played from one of our mirror servers guaranteeing all videos will be up and ready to watch.
There will also be a link to the original YouTube video if you are given a mirror.

Navigating DuelTube is simple, At the top of the page is a NavBar listing Season 1 - Season 4 and the home button that brings you back here.
Below those is an amusing sentence taken from random episodes and below that is another NavBar containing everything else, all you need to do is click on a category and then select an episode.

This site was made by two long time LittleKuriboh fans, we always joked about creating it when episode 29 "The Not-So-Super Roba Bros"was on and Tristan loads DuelTube up on his laptop to watch CardGame tournaments with serenity.. and then what was going to be a quick list of embedded episodes turned into a one month project in dreamweaver. we created this for all YuGiOh Abridged fans to enjoy with the hope of making a fun & easy way to find all of LittleKuriboh's best content.. even if YouTube removes something for whatever crazy reason. as well as that we will never put ads on this site, so please, Enjoy!



BasicYuGiOh Abridged Info

This is the story of Yugi Muto, a teenager who has yet to hit puberty. When Yugi unlocks the mysterious secrets of the Millenium Puzzle, an ancient spirit known only as the Pharaoh appears, and the pair embark on a journey to gain closure for the spirit. Along the way, Yugi and the Pharaoh face all manner of evil, from spoiled, dragon-obsessed millionaires to jealous, midriff-baring tomb-keepers, all with their own diabolical agendas. With the support of the Pharaoh, Yugi's love interest Téa, Brooklyn native Joey, and the ever-useless Tristan, Yugi takes on the toughest challenge of his life.

Oh. And occasionally, there's a children's card game.

DuelTubeFAQ


Q: Who made DuelTube?
A: DuelTubeWas created in Dreamweaver by rmellis and tallulah95 in the UK

Q: Why create DuelTube?
A: DuelTube was created for fun and to help organise YuGiOh Abridged

Q: Why do some videos need to switch to a mirror when they play just fine on youtube?
A: a lot of LittleKuriboh's videos have been flagged for stupid copyright reasons, even tho they are able to play on YouTube directly they can not be embedded some times, We will do our best to ensure that there are working backup mirrors at all time for those that wont play.

Q: How is this project funded?
A: It isn't, we used our own money and hosting to set this up ad free

Q: Why "dueltube.org" and not "dueltube.com" ?
