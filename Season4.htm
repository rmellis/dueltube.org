Season 4


Duel monsters begin wreaking havoc on the city and then Yugi and Yami discover a parallel world full of Duel Monsters, Kaiba must duel with Pegasus, but not all is as it seems and Mai returns, but there's something off about her.

Joey and Mai have a life-or-death duel and soon after the gang have to rescue Rebecca’s grandfather and Joey also learns that there are words on trading card..

 
Read More Yami duels against Rafael, Yugi also points out that there was NO ACTUAL STAKES for losing the duel, so losing would not have affected him revealing yet another plothole. Soon after The Obnoxious Celtic Guardian is summoned and The Pharaoh loses Yugi. He grieves in a valley with Téa, an old man with a stick named Ironheart, and Ironheart's granddaughter, Kris. Dartz uses Yami Yugi's vulnerability to plan an attack using the Orichalcos, which can make terrifying apparitions appear in the valley. The creature of the Orichalcos attacks Yami Yugi without regard to the rules of Duel Monsters. Ironheart and Kris attempt to help Yami Yugi, earning the wrath of Dartz. Yami Yugi wins the duel with the Orichalcos monster and reveals that Ironheart and Sky were spirits trapped in the valley. soon after The Pharaoh is attacked by birds! Getting stabbed by a Mirror Knight gives you seven years of bad luck and hospital bills. incase you didn’t know..
