Season 1


Our tale begins. Yugi/Yami and Kaiba face-off in a duel. Yugi then duels Pegasus over a cursed videotape and Grandpa loses his soul, Yugi and his friends take a relaxing boat ride to Duelist Kingdom but soon discover that they are not alone, Yugi and his friends are on Duelist Kingdom island looking for another duelist, and encounter Mai Valentine. After battling with her they then encounter a freaky fish guy with poor social skills.

 
Read More Yugi doesn't stand a ghost of a chance against his next opponent and Joey finally gets an episode to prove his worth... and fails miserably… Yugi must duel against an insane pyromaniac who likes to play children's card games, they then face off Bandith Keith, while Kaiba tries to save his brother Mokuba and joey takes on zombies… but he doesn’t stand a ghost of a chance! after some time Yugi takes on Kaiba.. again.. in a children’s card game of course, when night comes the minor characters come decide to seize their chance to shine and go off to have an adventure. the next day everyone's battling for a spot in the final round of Duelist Kingdom, but who will make it through? Surely it would never be Yugi, that would be way too obvious... Next a duel between Yugi and Pegasus commences, but they're not the only ones getting themselves into a bit of action. Pegasus is then greeted by an evil villain and Yugi receives a visit from a crazy Egyptian. and if that wasn’t enough.. Little girls, possessed teddy bears and card games! The next day the gang walk into an episode of Reboot when they are thrown into a virtual world in order to rescue Kaiba. and finely Duke Devlin is on the scene and he challenges Yugi to play his extremely original dice game.
