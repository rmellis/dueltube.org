DuelTube is an organised library of all the best YuGiOh Abridged content! It includes all 5 seasons and much more..We embed the official YouTube videos uploaded by LittleKuriboh within DuelTube to ensure that he is credited for videos watched on this site. If for some reason that video cannot be played here then it will be played from one of our mirror servers guaranteeing all videos will be up and ready to watch. There will also be a link to the original YouTube video if you are given a mirror.Navigating DuelTube is simple, At the top of the page is a NavBar listing Season 1 - Season 5 and the home button that brings you back here. Below those is an amusing sentence taken from random episodes and below that is another NavBar containing everything else, all you need to do is click on a category and then select an episode.This site was made by two long time LittleKuriboh fans, we always joked about creating it when episode 29 "The Not-So-Super Roba Bros"was on and Tristan loads DuelTube up on his laptop to watch CardGame tournaments with serenity.. and then what was going to be a quick list of embedded episodes turned into a one month project in dreamweaver. we created this for all YuGiOh Abridged fans to enjoy with the hope of making a fun & easy way to find all of LittleKuriboh's best content.. even if YouTube removes something for whatever crazy reason. as well as that we will never put ads on this site, so please, Enjoy!
